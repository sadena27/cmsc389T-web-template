Projects

Analysis on NYC Safety Data

I used ANOVA tests to determine statistical differences in service requests in different boroughs of NYC and created graphs with Matplotlib to show how the number of service requests varies over time and hour of day.

Honeypot Research Project

I built and deployed high interaction, container-based honeypots and wrote Bash/Python scripts to parse the data. I then conducted statistical analysis on the data to see how warning banners in varying languages affect attacker behavior.

Non-Fungible Turtles

I developed a full stack web application in collaboration with team members that randomly generates pixel art turtles available for purchase on OpenSea using a hash generated from the blockchain. We won best domain registered with Domain.com awarded by Major League Hacking at the Bitcamp hackathon.
